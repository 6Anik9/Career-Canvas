## 🖋️ The Vision
In a world of boring, black-and-white templates, **Career Canvas** is designed for the modern professional. We believe that a CV isn't just a list of jobs—it's a narrative of your growth. 

This project bridges the gap between **High-End Design** and **Functional Utility**, allowing users to build a professional identity that reflects the "Premium" standard of their work.

## ✨ Core Features
* 💎 **Cinematic Layouts:** Inspired by modern anime aesthetics and high-end editorial design.
* ✍️ **Narrative-First Design:** Tools to help you tell your story, not just list your skills.
* 🚀 **Instant Deployment:** Export your canvas to PDF or share it as a live digital portfolio.
* 🎨 **Custom Color Palettes:** Sophisticated presets (like our signature Crimson & Dark mode).
